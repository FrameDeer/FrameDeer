Mark Ruffalo

Date of Birth: November 22, 1967

Mark Ruffalo is an Academy Award-nominated actor known for his naturalistic performances and versatility across independent films and major blockbusters. Best known for playing Bruce Banner/The Hulk in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, he has also delivered critically acclaimed performances in numerous dramatic roles.

Major Awards

  • Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Television Movie – The Normal Heart (2014)
  • BAFTA Award for Best Actor in a Supporting Role – Spotlight (2016)
  • Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance by a Cast – Spotlight (2016)
  • Academy Award Nomination for Best Supporting Actor – The Kids Are All Right (2011)
  • Academy Award Nomination for Best Supporting Actor – Foxcatcher (2015)
  • Academy Award Nomination for Best Supporting Actor – Spotlight (2016)
  • Golden Globe Nomination for Best Supporting Actor – The Kids Are All Right (2011)
  • Golden Globe Nomination for Best Actor – The Normal Heart (2014)
