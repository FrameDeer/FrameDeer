Will Smith

Date of Birth: September 25, 1968

Will Smith is an Academy Award-winning actor, rapper, and producer who rose to fame as a hip-hop artist before becoming one of Hollywood's biggest movie stars. Known for his charismatic screen presence and ability to excel in both action blockbusters and dramatic roles, he has consistently been one of the most bankable stars in the industry.

Major Awards

  • Academy Award for Best Actor – King Richard (2022)
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Actor – Ali (2002)
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Actor – The Pursuit of Happyness (2007)
  • Screen Actors Guild Award for Best Actor – Ali (2002)
  • Grammy Award (4 wins)
  • NAACP Image Awards (multiple wins)
  • Academy Award Nomination for Best Actor – Ali (2002)
  • Academy Award Nomination for Best Actor – The Pursuit of Happyness (2007)
  • BAFTA Award Nomination for Best Actor – Ali (2002)
