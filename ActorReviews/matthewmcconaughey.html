Matthew McConaughey

Date of Birth: November 4, 1969

Matthew McConaughey is an Academy Award-winning actor known for his distinctive Southern charm and remarkable career transformation. After early success in romantic comedies, he underwent a career renaissance known as the "McConaissance" with critically acclaimed dramatic performances that established him as one of Hollywood's most respected actors.

Major Awards

  • Academy Award for Best Actor – Dallas Buyers Club (2014)
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Actor – Dallas Buyers Club (2014)
  • Screen Actors Guild Award for Best Actor – Dallas Buyers Club (2014)
  • Critics' Choice Movie Award for Best Actor – Dallas Buyers Club (2014)
  • MTV Movie Award for Best Male Performance – Dallas Buyers Club (2014)
  • Empire Award for Best Actor – Interstellar (2015)
