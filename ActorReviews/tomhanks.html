Tom Hanks

Date of Birth: July 9, 1956

Tom Hanks is one of Hollywood's most beloved and respected actors, known for his everyman charm and remarkable versatility. A two-time Academy Award winner for Best Actor, he has starred in some of the most iconic films of the past four decades, spanning comedy, drama, and historical epics.

Major Awards

  • Academy Award for Best Actor – Philadelphia (1994)
  • Academy Award for Best Actor – Forrest Gump (1995)
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Actor – Philadelphia (1994)
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Actor – Forrest Gump (1995)
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Actor – Cast Away (2001)
  • Screen Actors Guild Award for Best Actor – Forrest Gump (1995)
  • Screen Actors Guild Life Achievement Award (2024)
  • Presidential Medal of Freedom (2016)
  • Kennedy Center Honors (2014)
