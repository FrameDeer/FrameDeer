Morgan Freeman

Date of Birth: June 1, 1937

Morgan Freeman is an Academy Award-winning actor and narrator known for his distinctive voice and authoritative presence. With a career spanning over five decades, he has become one of Hollywood's most respected and recognizable figures, appearing in numerous critically acclaimed and commercially successful films.

Major Awards

  • Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor – Million Dollar Baby (2005)
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Actor – Driving Miss Daisy (1990)
  • Screen Actors Guild Award for Best Supporting Actor – Million Dollar Baby (2005)
  • Kennedy Center Honor (2008)
  • AFI Life Achievement Award (2011)
  • Screen Actors Guild Life Achievement Award (2018)
  • Academy Award Nomination for Best Actor – Driving Miss Daisy (1990)
  • Academy Award Nomination for Best Actor – The Shawshank Redemption (1995)
  • Academy Award Nomination for Best Supporting Actor – Street Smart (1988)
  • Academy Award Nomination for Best Supporting Actor – Invictus (2010)
