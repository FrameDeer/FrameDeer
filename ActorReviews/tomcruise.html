Tom Cruise

Date of Birth: July 3, 1962

Tom Cruise is one of Hollywood's most successful and recognizable actors, known for his intense dedication to performing his own stunts and his roles in blockbuster action films. With a career spanning over four decades, he has become one of the highest-grossing movie stars of all time and is particularly famous for his Mission: Impossible franchise.

Major Awards

  • Golden Globe Award for Best Actor – Born on the Fourth of July (1990)
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Actor – Jerry Maguire (1997)
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Supporting Actor – Magnolia (2000)
  • Academy Award Nomination for Best Actor – Born on the Fourth of July (1990)
  • Academy Award Nomination for Best Actor – Jerry Maguire (1997)
  • BAFTA Award Nomination for Best Actor – Jerry Maguire (1997)
  • Screen Actors Guild Award Nomination – Magnolia (2000)
  • Saturn Award for Best Actor – Mission: Impossible (1997)
