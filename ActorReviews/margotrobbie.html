Margot Robbie

Date of Birth: July 2, 1990

Nationality: Australian

Margot Robbie is an Academy Award-nominated actress and producer known for her versatility and captivating screen presence. Rising to international fame with her role in The Wolf of Wall Street, she has since established herself as one of Hollywood's most talented and bankable stars, particularly acclaimed for her portrayal of Harley Quinn in the DC Extended Universe.

Major Awards & Nominations

  • Academy Award Nomination for Best Actress – I, Tonya (2018)
  • Academy Award Nomination for Best Picture – Barbie (2024, as producer)
  • BAFTA Award Nomination for Best Actress – I, Tonya (2018)
  • Golden Globe Nomination for Best Actress – I, Tonya (2018)
  • Screen Actors Guild Award Nomination – Bombshell (2020)
  • Critics' Choice Movie Award for Best Actress in a Comedy – Barbie (2024)
  • Empire Award for Best Actress – I, Tonya (2018)
